When Things Happen — Recruiting Timeline by Grade
Know what coaches expect at each stage so you're never behind
Grade
What You Should Be Doing
Freshman
Build highlight film. Play varsity. Get grades right. No coach outreach yet.
Sophomore
Start attending showcases. Build your target school list. Connect on social media with coaches.
Junior (Fall)
Begin official outreach. Email coaches. D1 coaches can now contact you directly.
Junior (Spring)
Unofficial visits. Verbal offers come in. Attend Perfect Game, WWBA. Follow up aggressively.
Senior (Fall)
Early signing period: Nov 13-20. Official visits. Compare scholarship offers carefully.
Senior (Spring)
Late signing period: April. NLI signing. FAFSA deadline Dec 1. Enroll by May 1.
JUCO note: JUCO recruiting moves faster — coaches make decisions in weeks, not months. If you're going the JUCO route, contact coaches immediately and follow up within 7 days every time.

Common Questions
Does the Autopilot actually send emails on my behalf?
No — nothing sends without your approval. Every email goes into a review queue first. You read it, approve it, and then it marks ready to send. You stay in full control of what goes out.
Will coaches know it was written by AI?
No. Every email is personalized with the coach's name, the program's conference, and specific recruiting notes. It reads like a thoughtful, individually written email — because the AI treats each one that way.
Can I switch back to Basic at any time?
Yes. You can cancel or downgrade anytime. No contracts, no commitment.
What's the difference between writing one email vs the Autopilot?
The Email Writer generates one email at a time for a specific school. Autopilot scans the entire database, matches programs to your player's profile, and generates personalized emails for all of them at once — 12+ emails in about 30 seconds.
Is there a free trial?
Yes — 7 days free, no credit card required. Try the full Autopilot experience before you pay anything.
